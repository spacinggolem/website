Memento Mori:

Memento Mori is a gaming community founded in August, 2011. What was originally a small group of players has grown over the years into a diverse gaming community. We provide a friendly, mature group of gamers that play a variety of games and are very active! We're always looking to broaden our reach and branch into different games as well as different regions of the world. Come check us out today!
