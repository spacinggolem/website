Patch notes V1.41!

Psyonix announced the new update that will be coming with the start of the new season! This update will include the season rewards and a new crate. It will also include a soft reset, wich means you will have to play 10 placement matches in every gamemode to get your rank back. For further information, visit Psyonix's website .
